Patient: I applied ice twice yesterday and tried to rest as much as possible.

Patient: I took ibuprofen once, and it helped a little.

Doctor: Your blood pressure is 122/78 mmHg, pulse is 76 beats per minute, and your temperature is normal. On examination, there is mild swelling around the right knee with tenderness along the medial aspect. Range of motion is slightly limited due to pain, but the knee is stable.
